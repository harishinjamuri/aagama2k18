Mirror Walk

About the Event

The participant will be provided with a mirror of normal size,
by looking into the mirror the person must walk reverse in the path provided,
the person who completes the task with the least time will be announced as a winner
RULES :-
● Only one individual can play the game
● He/She should not turn head during the game
● He/She must walk reverse by looking into the mirror only
