Words Worth

About the Event

We Have all participants come with a paper and display one big word on the screen at a time. Participants have 60 seconds to write as many small words from the one big word as possible in those 60 seconds.Repeat for at least 5 rounds. The one with highest word count of legitimate words (with letters only from the one big word) wins!
