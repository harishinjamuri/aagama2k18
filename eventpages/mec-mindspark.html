About the Event

An event wherein participants will be quizzed on topics from the engineering world and more! The quiz involves pictures, words, descriptions and choosing correct options all
revolving around technology. The difficulty increases with each level and it is the survival of the SMARTEST!

Rules :
1. The individual participants will register and each phase will contain 6 participants.
2. Each level will have 15 questions with words, pictures etc and participant with LEAST points will be eliminated. There will be 5 levels and every successive level gets difficult.
3. The final round will be a one on one rapid fire round and the participant with maximum points wins.
4. On day 2, the winners from day one will participate in the grand finale and we will have new phases too.
No . of days of event : 2
