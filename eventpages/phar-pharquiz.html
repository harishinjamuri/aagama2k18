PHARMA QUIZ

About the Event

Rules:
1.  Each team will comprise of 3 candidates.
2.  The quiz will have 1 Elimination round and 3 final rounds.
3.  4 teams will be selected for final round.
4.  The last date of receiving the registration fee is 15th March 2018
5.  Elimination : This will consist of 15 objective questions which have to be solved in 5 minutes. 4 highest scoring teams will compete in the finals.
6.  The last 3 rounds will be played among 4 teams.
7.  Finals : Consists of 3 rounds
     1.  First round: General questions and Visual Round
     2.  Second round: Buzzer Round
     3.  Third round: Rapid Fire Round
