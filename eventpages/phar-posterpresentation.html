About the Event

1.  A team should contain a maximum of 2 members . Submit an abstract with not more than 200 words, on or before 12:00PM 15th March, 2018.

3.  Send the abstract to aagama2k18pharmacy@cvsr.ac.in with the subject of format -‘Abstract –your id. no – your name’.

10.  Topics are: Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical analysis, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Regulatory affairs, Hospital Pharmcy,Industrial Pharmacy.
