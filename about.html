“Anurag Group of Institutions is organising Aagama2k18
on 16th and 17th March, 2018”



Graduation and Post-graduation involves more practical than theoretical knowledge, the exposure to the advanced scenario is attained only through participation of various external events exchanging culture and knowledge, as young minds evolve to take on the external world.

As a part of Aagama, the technical papers are invited across country. Many other events like Tech Quiz,Virtual job fair, Poster presentation, Project Presentation, Circuitrix, Velocity, Inversion, Model Making, Lan Gaming and many more are included to extract the inherent talent of young minds. This two day technical fest is right platform for students to improve communication skills, exchange their views, ideas and update their technical knowledge in present engineering scenario.
